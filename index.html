About Me

Casey received her B.A. in Economics and Linguistics in June 2015 from Northwestern University. She currently manages the Child Language Development Lab at Northwestern University, where her focus has shifted toward mobile development and R. She is interested in how the ChiLDlab can use these tools to improve and automate data capture, visualization, and sharing.

As a jazz/latin pianist and drummer, when not in the lab Casey enjoys making music, exploring Chicago's jazz scene, and learning to DJ.
